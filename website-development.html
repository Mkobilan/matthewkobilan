Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of your project. A basic website might take 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects with custom functionality can take 6-12 weeks. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a more specific timeline based on your requirements.

How much does a website cost?

Pricing varies based on your specific needs, project complexity, and timeline. I offer custom quotes tailored to each project rather than fixed packages. This ensures you get exactly what you need without paying for unnecessary features. Contact me for a personalized quote.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites I build are fully responsive and optimized for all devices, including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers. This ensures your visitors have a great experience no matter how they access your site.

Do you offer website maintenance?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance and support packages to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and running smoothly. This includes regular updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, and content updates as needed.

Can you help with SEO?

Yes, all websites I build include basic SEO optimization to help you rank better in search engines. I also offer more comprehensive SEO services including keyword research, content optimization, technical SEO, and ongoing optimization strategies.
